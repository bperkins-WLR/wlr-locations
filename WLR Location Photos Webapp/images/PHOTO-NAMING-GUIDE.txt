─── TO ADD OR UPDATE A PHOTO ───────────────────────────────────
  1. Find the right loc-## folder inside images/
  2. Name your photo 01.jpg (exterior) or 02.jpg (interior)
  3. Drop it into that folder
  4. Re-deploy the WLR Location Photos Webapp folder to Netlify
  5. It automatically updates on Randy's phone!

─── TIPS ────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
  • JPG format (smaller file size, fast loading on mobile)
  • Recommended size: 800×600 pixels minimum
  • Landscape orientation looks best
  • Keep under 500KB per photo
